## 1. Company Overview
- **Company Name:** Boss Cargo Express Freight Services Inc. (BCE)
- **Established:** 2014
- **Origin:** Started in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan.
- **Slogan:** "Synergy beyond compare."
- **Core Message:** Boss Cargo Express is focused on building partnerships that inspire growth. We take pride in what we do and we will always be there when you need and where you need us the most.

### The Boss Cargo Express Story
Founded in 2014, Boss Cargo Express started its roots in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan. Since then, the company has grown and delivered the best cargo solutions to various clients across a wide range of industries across the Philippine archipelago. Our team consists of skilled professionals with years of solid experience in handling ground freight, sea freight, and air freight.

### Our Journey (Key Milestones)
- **2014: Company Founded** - Started in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan, with a vision to deliver premium cargo solutions across the archipelago.
- **2015-2018: Growth & Expansion** - Expanded operations nationwide and built a team of experts in ground, sea, and air freight.
- **2019-2022: Strategic Development** - Established corporate directives to add value to clients and strengthened functional areas for high competitiveness.
- **2023-Present: Technology & Sustainability** - Focused on supply chain technology adoption, geographical expansion, and serving wider market segments through effective resource management.

## 2. Mission, Vision, and Values
### Our Mission
To grow and empower businesses across the Philippines by providing customized logistics solutions and seek to develop a highly sustainable business by using state-of-the-art technology and creating unprecedented value and opportunity for our customers and employees while embracing the frame of **CANI (Constant And Never Ending Improvement)**.

### Our Dream (Vision)
To be the country's preeminent and technologically driven logistics company.

### Brand Values
- **Transparent Communication:** Focus on openness and clarity in all interactions.
- **Business Sustainability:** Commitment to long-term impact and sustainable practices.
- **Continuous Learning:** Embracing growth and education (supported by Boss Cargo University).
- **Cohesive Teamwork:** Working as one unit towards the common goal of client growth.

### Corporate Philosophy
The heart of Boss Cargo Express is centered on businesses. We understand that companies have top priorities to stay competitive and financially sound. We encourage businesses to outsource their logistics requirements to us so they can focus on their core operations.

## 3. Services Portfolio
Boss Cargo Express provides a holistic range of cargo solutions ideally fitted to client needs.

### International Freight Forwarding
- **Air Freight:** Global air transport solutions.
- **Sea Freight:** Full Cargo Load (FCL) and Loose Cargo Load (LCL).
- **Brokerage Services:**
  - Import and Export Customs Clearance.
  - International Trade & Tariff Classification.
  - Licenses and Permits facilitation.
  - PEZA (Philippine Economic Zone Authority) facilitation.

### Domestic Services
- **Air Freight:** Rapid domestic delivery.
- **Land Freight:** Extensive trucking and ground transport.
- **Sea Freight:** FCL and LCL options across the archipelago.
- **RORO (Roll-on/Roll-off):** Specialized transport for wheeled cargo.

### Warehousing and Distribution
- Strategic storage solutions and supply chain management.

### Value-Added Services
- Professional Packing and Crating.
- Specialized Permits (e.g., for live animals and frozen meats).

## 5. Strategic Partnerships & Industries
BCE acts as an extension of the client's team, serving critical roles in the movement and storage of goods.

### Industries Served
- **Business Process Outsourcing (BPO):** Logistics support for high-paced corporate environments.
- **Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG):** Rapid distribution for essential consumer products.
- **Engineering Services:** Transport and logistics for technical equipment and materials.
- **Food Services:** Specialized handling for temperature-sensitive and perishable goods.
- **Financial Services:** Secure logistics for the banking and finance sector.
- **Retail:** End-to-end supply chain solutions for the retail industry.

### Memberships & Accreditations
- **Supply Chain Management Association of the Philippines (SCMAP):** Active member of the premier supply chain and logistics association in the country.
- **Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA):** Accredited partner for PEZA facilitation services, supporting economic zone operations.
- **JCtrans Network:** Premium Member of this international freight forwarders network, providing global logistics connectivity.
