### The Boss Cargo Express Story
Founded in 2014, Boss Cargo Express started its roots in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan. Since then, the company has grown and delivered the best cargo solutions to various clients across a wide range of industries across the Philippine archipelago. Our team consists of skilled professionals with years of solid experience in handling ground freight, sea freight, and air freight.

Boss Cargo Express recognizes the potential changes in the macroenvironment of the wider transportation and storage sector. Management continuously prepares the firm amidst industry and customer trends. Coming up with corporate directives and initiatives that add value to existing and potential clients, strengthening functional areas, and making the corporation highly competitive are top priorities. 

### Our Journey & Strategy
- **2014: Company Founded** - Started in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan, with a vision to deliver premium cargo solutions across the archipelago.
- **Growth & Expansion** - Expanded operations nationwide, building a team of experts in ground, sea, and air freight.
- **Strategic Development** - Established corporate directives to add value to clients and strengthened functional areas for high competitiveness.
- **Technology & Sustainability** - Focused on supply chain technology adoption, infrastructural developments, and economic growth. Key strategies encompass extending product scope, expanding geographical locations, and serving wider market segments through effective resource management and sound financial planning.

## 3. Services Portfolio
Being part of the entire value chain, BCE plays a crucial role in adding value to customers. It is deeper than delivering parcels via trucks or motorcycles—it is about comprehensive logistics and supply chain management.

### International Freight Forwarding
- **Air Freight:** Global air transport solutions.
- **Sea Freight:** Full Cargo Load (FCL) and Loose Cargo Load (LCL).
- **Brokerage Services:**
  - Import and Export Customs Clearance.
  - International Trade & Tariff Classification.
  - Licenses and Permits facilitation.
  - PEZA (Philippine Economic Zone Authority) facilitation.

### Domestic Services
- **Air Freight:** Rapid domestic delivery.
- **Land Freight / Trucking Services:** Extensive trucking and ground transport.
- **Sea Freight:** Full Cargo Load (FCL) and Loose Cargo Load (LCL).
- **RORO (Roll-on/Roll-off):** Specialized transport for wheeled cargo.

### Warehousing and Distribution
- Strategic storage solutions and supply chain management.

### Value-Added Services
- Professional Packing and Crating.
- Specialized Permits (e.g., for live animals and frozen meats).
